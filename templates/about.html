Hi there, Ethglobal & Skale!

Today, banks are dominating international trade through what is called Letter of Credit. Despite process improvements on their side, the field has seen little innovation over the past decades.

"This is how it's always been done. (Note also, we control the relationship with our clients.)"

As a consequence, lots of companies are exempt from appropriate financing tools that make trade with strangers more secure and dependable.

Our project is making the attempt to take them out of the equation and make finance more accessible.

Our idea was to implement a system consisting of three parties:

  • Exporter (seller)
  • Importer (buyer)
  • Freight forwarder (shipping company)

This is similar to the current setup, except without banks in between.
